Sandwich


A Sandwich is always quick, delicious and healthy.

I'll show you my favourite sandwich and how to prepare it!

Ingredients

  • Your favourite salty bread
  • 2 slices of jam
  • 1 slice of melting cheese
  • 3 slices of red tomato
  • 3 slices of onion
  • 1 egg
  • Mayo and mustard

Instructions

  1. Cut the bread in two.
  2. Spread mayo in both parts of the bread.
  3. Put one of the parts of bread, then one slice of cheese and one slice of jam.
  4. Cook the egg and add it to the sandwich.
  5. Put the slices of tomato and onion, the other slice of jam and the other slice of cheese.
  6. Heat both sides of the sandwich in a pan until it gets crunchy
  7. Open it and add mustard.
  8. Enjoy!
