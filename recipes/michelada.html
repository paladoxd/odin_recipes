Michelada


Michelada is the most popular beer-based cocktail in Mexico.

Give your favourite lager beer an amazing and really mexican twist, mexicans, we love lemon and chile, why not add it to a beer?

Ingredients

  • 2 lemons
  • Your favourite very cold lager beer 355ml/12oz (If you want a mexican one, Pacifico is the best option)
  • 10ml Hot Sauce
  • 5ml Worcestershire Sauce
  • Salt
  • Chile powder (Taj√≠n or Chile Piquin)

Instructions

  1. Cut the lemons and squeeze three halfs in a little bowl.
  2. Mix the salt and the chile powder in a plate.
  3. With one of the squeezeed half, wet the edge of the glass and dip it in the plate with salt.
  4. Add the juice, both sauces and the beer in the glass.
  5. Cut a slice of lemon and use it as decoration in the glass.
  6. Enjoy!
  7. Don't get wasted :)
